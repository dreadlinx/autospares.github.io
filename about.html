About Mobile Truck Clinic

Who We Are

Mobile Truck Clinic is a proudly South African company established in 2016. We specialize in heavy-duty truck repairs, engine rebuilds, and supplying top-quality spare parts for trucks, buses, and LDVs.

What We Do

We work with well-known vehicle brands like Volvo, Mercedes-Benz, Scania, and ZF, ensuring reliability and quality in every service.
